Building Clarity
in a Noisy World

Jim Read is a strategic refiner who lives at the intersection of strategy, people, and process. With a career spanning over 25 years at ConocoPhillips, Jim navigated the complexities of engineering, finance, and global talent development. His versatility as a 'build-it / fix-it' leader spans the strategic spectrum, from managing $1B asset development programs to building a Global Learning Center of Excellence, always with a focus on turning organizational noise into strategic clarity.

Integrative Thinking for an AI Era. Jim's background as an Engineer and Finance leader has made him a highly adaptable, inquisitive, and integrative thinker. He is adept at mastering unfamiliar content and translating it into actionable frameworks. As a strong and empathetic leader, Jim understands that while technology redefines work, human capability and team performance determine whether that change elevates performance or erodes it.

The Uncharted Mission. Jim founded Uncharted Insights to help leaders and organizations build the human core capabilities required to thrive alongside AI. He believes that in an automated world, the most valuable assets are the uniquely human ones: judgment, meaning-making, and trust. Jim's mission is to ensure that as technology scales, human insight remains the ultimate competitive advantage.
